How It Works
01

SPEAK

Say any command naturally. "Balloon bridge." "Defend left." "Queue hog rider."

02

ROUTE

The command router picks the fastest execution path in under 5ms. Simple commands stay fully on-device.

03

PLAY

Your card plays in the game. Fast Path: 170ms. Smart Path: under 1 second. Queue Path: auto-plays on card cycle.

Three Tiers of Intelligence

FAST PATH

~170ms

Say the card and the zone. That's it. The entire pipeline runs on-device — speech recognition, fuzzy matching, hand detection, gesture injection. No network. No cloud. No waiting. Your card plays before you finish lowering your hand.

FAST PATH Demo

QUEUE PATH

Auto-Play

Pre-program your next play. Say 'queue balloon bridge' and the AI watches your hand cycle. The moment balloon appears in your hand, it auto-plays. Chain commands: 'knight left then archers right' executes a full offensive sequence on autopilot.

QUEUE PATH Demo

SMART PATH

~800ms

Don't know which card to play? Just say what you want. 'Defend against hog rider.' Claude Opus pre-analyzes your deck before the match. Gemini Flash uses that playbook to make pro-level decisions in under a second. It picks the card, picks the placement, and tells you why.
